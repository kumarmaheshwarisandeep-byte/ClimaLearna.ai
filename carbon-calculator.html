Understanding Scopes

Emissions Classification

The GHG Protocol defines three scopes for comprehensive carbon accounting. Click a scope to open its calculator above.

🔌

Scope 1 — Direct Emissions

Company-owned sources including fleet vehicles, on-site fuel combustion, and refrigerant leakage tracking. These are emissions you control directly.

Open Calculator →
⚡

Scope 2 — Indirect Energy

Purchased electricity, steam, heating, and cooling with location-based and market-based calculation methods reflecting grid emission factors.

Open Calculator →
🚚

Scope 3 — Value Chain

15 categories of value chain emissions including purchased goods, transportation, business travel, and product use phase — often the largest source.
