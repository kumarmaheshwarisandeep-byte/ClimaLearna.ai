## 3. Logo Concept Ideas

### Concept A: "Leaf Node"
A minimalist leaf constructed from a geometric neural node pattern. The leaf is formed by connected data points and lines, suggesting both nature and machine learning. The ".ai" in the logotype is subtly highlighted in the accent color. Clean, monoline, works as both an icon and a favicon.

### Concept B: "Circuit Leaf"
A single continuous line drawing that starts as a circuit path and curves into a leaf silhouette. Represents the fusion of technology and environment. Paired with a clean sans-serif wordmark where "Clima" and "Learn" have distinct weights. No shapes, no boxes — just pure line work.

### Concept C: "Carbon Pulse"
A minimalist wave/pulse icon inspired by both carbon concentration curves and neural network activations. Three subtle vertical bars that resemble both a carbon trend chart and abstract leaves. Extremely minimal — works at 16px favicon size. Wordmark uses a modern geometric sans-serif with subtle tracking.

## 5. Typography System

### Headings
- **Font:** Inter (sans-serif), weight 700–800
- **Fallback:** system-ui, -apple-system, sans-serif
- **Letter spacing:** -0.02em for H1, -0.01em for H2-H3
- **Line height:** 1.15–1.25
- **Style:** Clean, slightly tight, modern SaaS

### Body
- **Font:** Inter (sans-serif), weight 400–500
- **Fallback:** system-ui, -apple-system, sans-serif
- **Line height:** 1.6
- **Size:** 16px–18px for desktop, 15px–16px for mobile
- **Style:** Highly readable, neutral, no serifs

### Monospace (data/code)
- **Font:** JetBrains Mono or SF Mono
- **Usage:** Carbon numbers, data points, API references

**Why Inter:** Designed for screens, excellent readability at all sizes, used by Stripe, Vercel, and GitHub. Clean, modern, engineering-friendly.

## 6. UI Style Guide

### Buttons
- **Primary:** Solid `#1B6B3A` fill, white text, 8px border-radius, 14px 32px padding
- **Hover:** Darken to `#0A2E1E`, subtle shadow lift (0 8px 25px rgba(27,107,58,0.3))
- **Secondary/Outline:** Transparent fill, 2px `#1B6B3A` stroke, `#1B6B3A` text
- **Size:** 44px minimum touch target, 14px–16px font weight 600
- **Transition:** 0.3s ease all

### Cards
- **Background:** White
- **Border:** 1px solid `#E5E7EB`
- **Radius:** 12px
- **Shadow:** 0 4px 20px rgba(0,0,0,0.06)
- **Hover:** Shadow deepens to 0 8px 30px rgba(0,0,0,0.1), border shifts to `#95D5B2`
- **Padding:** 32px 24px

### Spacing & Layout
- **Page max-width:** 1200px
- **Section padding:** 80px top/bottom (desktop), 48px (mobile)
- **Grid gaps:** 24px
- **Content padding:** 24px on sides
- **Hero padding:** 140px top, 80px bottom

### Design Principles
1. **Content-first:** White space is a feature. Let content breathe.
2. **Minimal chrome:** No unnecessary borders, shadows, or decorations.
3. **One primary action per section:** Every section has one clear CTA.
4. **Consistent radius:** 8px for buttons, 12px for cards, 20px for hero shapes.
5. **Subtle animations:** Float effects, fade-in on scroll, hover lifts. Nothing aggressive.

## 7. Brand Voice & Tone

### How ClimaLearn.ai Speaks
We sound like a smart, focused engineer who happens to care deeply about the planet. Confident but not arrogant. Technical but accessible. We let the data speak.

### Words to Use
- Intelligence, engine, analytics, track, model, measure, automate
- Engineers, professionals, practitioners, analysts
- Carbon, ESG, HSE, LEED, Estidama, PQP, compliance, green building
- AI-powered, ML-driven, data-first, real-time, streamlined

### Words to Avoid
- Global leader, pioneering, end-to-end solutions, world-class services
- Consultancy, advisory firm, partner (in the consulting sense)
- Carbon offset certification provider, eco-friendly badge
- Guru, ninja, rockstar (overused startup clichés)

### Tone Guidelines
| Quality | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Confident** | We know our domain. We don't hedge. |
| **Direct** | Short sentences. No filler. |
| **Technical but clear** | Engineering vocabulary, explained simply. |
| **Modern** | Startup energy. Not corporate. Not academic. |
| **Grounded** | No greenwashing. No exaggeration. Data speaks. |
